About University

Pandit Deendayal Energy University’s 100-acre campus is located in Gandhinagar, which is the capital city of Gujarat, and is located 23 Km north of a well-developed city called Ahmedabad, with a population of 8 million people. The city is famous for its remarkable cultural development and social life.

PDEU offers multiple courses ranging from engineering, arts, and management along with maximum exposure and opportunities to its students through various national and international exchange programs with the best universities worldwide. For the development of its faculties and staff, the university endeavors various joint exchange and research programs. Official PDEU About Page

Unique Features

  • Emphasis on problem solving and practice with sound fundamentals, through active and continued partnership with energy & infrastructure sector companies and National and International Universities, Consultants, and Vendors.
  • Top down approach from ‘Systems to Components’ to better appreciate holistic education.
  • Rural, Industry, and Research internships to provide a better understanding of challenges faced in rural and urban Indian landscapes, industry culture, and career options for students.
  • Emphasis on holistic engineering as key to the long-term economic and environmental sustainability of communities.

Infrastructure

Library

Library is basically a collection of sources, resources and services. The Library and Information Centre here extensively utilizes Information Technology to ensure the best source and information reach to the user. The library has been automated using Alice for Windows (AfW) - an international user-friendly library package. The software facilitates automated circulation (issue-return) of books and speedy access to bibliographic, location, and availability information of books in the library.

The library has:

  • 5520 books
  • 44 journals
  • 141 CDs
  • All kinds of newspapers

The Lecture Hall

Air-conditioned lecture halls of the university provide a comfortable environment for students to learn and develop. All lecture halls are equipped with projectors and PCs and have comfortable seating with rotatable chairs. The university has around 10 lecture halls dedicated to the School of Liberal Studies.

Cafeteria & Food Court

The university has ensured that students enjoy a healthy diet charted out by a dietitian. The food court provides wholesome and nutritious meals and refreshments throughout the day. It offers an eco-friendly environment where students can relax while deliberating on academic challenges.

Performing Arts Room

The Performing Arts Room is designed to keep culture alive in the School of Liberal Studies (SLS). Students prepare and practice for dance, music, drama, and other cultural activities. The room is equipped with mirrors on one wall and open space for practice. Theatre, music, and dance classes are also conducted here.
